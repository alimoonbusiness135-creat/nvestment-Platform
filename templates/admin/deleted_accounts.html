GDPR Compliance

Account deletion records are kept for compliance and security purposes. These records contain minimal identifying information and are automatically purged after 180 days from deletion.

According to GDPR regulations, users have the right to be forgotten, and our system complies by:

  • Permanently removing all personal and financial data
  • Keeping minimal records for security and fraud prevention
  • Automatically purging deletion records after the required retention period
Important: Do not attempt to restore deleted accounts or contact users after account deletion unless required by law enforcement.
